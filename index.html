Thanks for visiting!

We hope you can find everything you need in our little, local and organic Speech Garden! The Speech Garden is focused on providing high-quality speech and language services to Brooklynites - we will do everything we can to help your child excel!

We would love the opportunity to talk with you about your child's unique needs. We pride ourselves in creating positive, supportive and motivating environments where children blossom. We work with caregivers to to create mutually agreed upon goals. Brooklyn's Speech Garden is locally based and therapist owned and operated for that oh so special Brooklyn touch. You know that your concerns will be heard here.

Brooklyn's Speech Garden offers speech, language and academic testing. Many children experience difficulty functioning in home, school or social environments, have trouble being understood, communicating effectively, or learning at their grade level. Assessment addresses not only a child's needs, but also identifies strengths to help them compensate and succeed! For children who are experiencing communication and learning difficulties, we provide quality one-on-one and group therapy. The Speech Garden designs individual treatment programs and carefully evaluates these programs on an ongoing basis in order to make changes depending on your child's progress. We consult with teachers and caregivers to ensure children carryover skills into all environments.

The Speech Garden prides itself on making your search for a local, quality speech therapist as easy as possible. Look around our website. If you have any questions or concerns about your child's communication development or performance in school we do hope you give us a call to chat!
